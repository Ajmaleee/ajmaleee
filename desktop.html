Designer. Developer.

I'm Ajmal Ali, a passionate frontend developer and UI/UX enthusiast from Kerala, India. I build beautiful, fast, and responsive websites — the kind that make people stop and say "wow, who made this?"

My work lives at the intersection of clean code and great design. I pay obsessive attention to detail — from how a hover animation feels, to how a layout breathes on mobile. Every pixel has a reason.

I've built everything from macOS-in-the-browser simulations, to electronics storefronts, AI assistant UIs, and GPU stress-test tools. Each project taught me something new, and I'm always hungry for the next challenge.
